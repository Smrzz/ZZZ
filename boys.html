boys (stylized as $BOY$) is an American hip hop duo from New Orleans, Louisiana. Formed in 2014 by cousins Scrim (stylized as $crim) and Ruby da Cherry, the duo initially rose to popularity on SoundCloud for their abrasive, self-produced beats, as well as their harsh lyrical content and themes prominently featuring substance abuse and suicidal ideation.
