Aristos Petrou is an American rapper and musical artist. He is better known by his stage name Ruby Da Cherry. In 2014, he formed the rap group boys (stylised spelling: $BOY$) along with his cousin Scott Arceneaux Jr. (stage name: $lick or $crim). A native of New Orleans, Aristos was deeply influenced by the rich musical culture of his city and was inspired to make music at an early age. However, his youth was marked by drug addiction and poverty.
