Scott Anthony Arceneaux Jr., known professionally as Scrim (stylized $crim), is an American rapper born on April 11, 1989 New Orleans, Louisiana. He is best known for being one half of the rap duo boys (stylized $boy$) along with his cousin, Ruby da Cherry.
